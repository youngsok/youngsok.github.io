Biography

I am currently an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Yonsei University.

Before joining Yonsei University, I was a post-doc researcher at Seoul National University. I earned my PhD and BSc in Computer Science and Engineering from POSTECH under the supervision of Prof. Jangwoo Kim. During my PhD studies, I worked as an intern at Google Mountain View office and Samsung Electronics.

To prospective students: I am looking for motivated students interested in computer architecture and systems research. Please visit the website for my lab to see what research topics I am actively pursuing.

Research Interests

  • Computer architecture and systems
  • GPU architecture for computer graphics and general-purpose computing
  • Architectural and system-level support for artificial intelligence
  • Processor performance modeling
  • FPGA-based acceleration
